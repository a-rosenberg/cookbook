banana banana pancakes

ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 3 eggs
  • Some sort of flour (I use almond or white wheat flour)
  • 1 scant tsp baking powder
  • salt (optional)
  • coconut oil
  • toppings; chocolate chips, blueberries (even frozen), peanut butter

instructions

  1. Smash bananas until they’re pudding. Mix in the eggs. Add flour until good pancake consistency (maybe 1/4 to 1/2 cup). Add baking powder and stir or whisk in. Add a pinch of salt if desired.
  2. Heat medium (~12”) pan over medium heat. Once hot add a bit of coconut oil and melt. Use a 1/4 cup scoop to add 3 pancakes to the pan, or use a tablespoon and make silver dollar pancakes.
  3. Cook a couple of minutes either side making sure they’re not burning. Once they’re nicely browned remove the pancakes, add a bit more oil and start again - until batter is gone.

notes

Frozen wild blueberries are the best topping.
