banana oat muffins

breakfast

ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened, plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup melted butter or oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 scant tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Extra rolled oats for topping
  • 12 muffin cups

instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Line a 12-cup muffin tray with muffin cups.
  2. In a blender, combine all ingredients except the extra oats for topping. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour the batter into the muffin cups, filling each about 3/4 full.
  4. Sprinkle a few extra rolled oats on top of each muffin.
  5. Bake for 18 minutes, or until just starting to brown. A toothpick test would also work.
  6. Remove muffins from the tray and allow to cool.

notes

Comes together quickly and easily. If bananas aren't very ripe you can add maple syrup but I like them more bread than cake-like.
