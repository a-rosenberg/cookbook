beet smoothie

ingredients

  • 2 small beets (or 1 medium/large); cooked and peeled
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk

instructions

  1. Combine.
  2. Blend until smooth. (water drop sign on a Vitamix)

notes

- Tastes like earth. - I like to use the precooked and peeled beets from Costco. - Can sneak in some flax meal as well.
