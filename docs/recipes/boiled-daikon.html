boiled daikon

ingredients

  • 1 lb (16 oz) daikon; peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup water or stock
  • 1 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • (optional) 1 slice ginger
  • (optional) 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp sugar or honey
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper; 1 pinch
  • 1 green onion; chopped
  • 1 splash sesame oil; once cooked

instructions

  1. Combine water (or stock), oyster sauce, ginger salt, sugar and white pepper in pop and stir to combine.
  2. Bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce to a simmer over medium-low and cook covered for 20 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and garnish with green onion and sesame oil. Mix well and serve as a side.

notes

Congee topping.
