brussels pierogies

dinner

ingredients

  • 1–1.5 lbs frozen or refrigerated cheese and potato pierogies
  • 1 onion; thinly sliced
  • 8 oz Brussels sprouts; thinly sliced
  • 2–4 Tbsp butter
  • salt, for boiling water and seasoning
  • olive oil, for cooking (optional)

instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat and melt 2 Tbsp of the butter.
  3. Add the sliced onion and cook until it just begins to turn golden. Add the sliced Brussels sprouts and continue cooking until they’re tender, adding more butter or a splash of olive oil if the pan starts to dry out.
  4. Once the water is boiling, add the perogies and cook until they float and are heated through (per package instructions).
  5. Drain the perogies and transfer them to the skillet. Add the remaining butter, toss to coat and lightly brown the perogies.
  6. Season with salt to taste and serve immediately.

notes

Seasoning salt is good option for finishing the dish.
