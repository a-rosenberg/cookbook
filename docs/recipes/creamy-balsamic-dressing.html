creamy balsamic dressing

salad

ingredients

  • 6 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 6 Tbsp avocado mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 3 small cloves garlic; minced

instructions

  1. Whisk or shake together in a large mason jar.

notes
