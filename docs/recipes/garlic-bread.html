garlic bread

italian

ingredients

  • 1 loaf whole wheat French bread
  • 4 Tbsp butter; softened at room temp
  • 4 cloves of garlic; minced
  • 2 tbsp parsley; minced
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Cut French bread in half (you should have two open sides).
  2. Mash together garlic, butter, parsley, and salt/pepper. Spread evenly on open side of bread. Sprinkle cheese on top.
  3. Bake 8–12 min until Parmesan is beginning to brown and bread is crunchy

notes

Dip in soup.
