simple soba noodles

japanese lunch

ingredients

  • 200g (~5-6 oz) dried soba noodles; 2 bunches
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce/shoyu
  • 1.5 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp grated ginger; don’t need to peel if it’s fresh
  • 2-3 green onion; thinly sliced
  • toasted white sesame seeds; for garnish
  • chili oil crunch
  • pickled raddish
  • runny egg
  • seared extra-firm tofu
  • edamame

instructions

  1. Put water up to boil. Combine shoyu, sesame oil, rice vinegar, ginger and sliced scallion in a cup. Add a splash or water or dashi.
  2. Once soba noodles are barely cooked through, strain and rinse under cold water. Place in bowl and top with sauce and garnishes. That’s it.

notes

Maybe more ginger. Serves 1 as a large meal, 2 as small. Could drop to 150g for a single portion. Good side for a larger Japanese meal.
