tofu chocolate mousse

ingredients

  • 1 package silken tofu drained (12 or 14 oz)
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate
  • 1 pinch kosher salt
  • 2 Tbsp maple syrup

instructions

  1. Blend everything really well - until the chocolate is no longer grainy.
  2. Put into storage and serving jars or bowl, then place in the fridge for a few hours. The flavor improves over time.

notes

Toppings; e.g., mint, raspberries, sliced almonds, shaved chocolate. I like storing and serving these in small mason jars.
