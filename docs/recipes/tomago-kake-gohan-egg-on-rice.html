tomago kake gohan (egg on rice)

japanese quick breakfast

ingredients

  • 1-2 cups cooked short or medium grain white rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp shoyu
  • 1 tsp mirin
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 smaller pinch MSG
  • (optional) asparagus
  • (optional) nori
  • (optional) furikake
  • (optional) diced kimchi

instructions

  1. Heat white rice until piping hot - 1.5 minutes in our microwave.
  2. Make a small hole in the middle of the rice and crack in egg and top with the rest of the non-optional ingredients.
  3. Whisk in with chopsticks and top with the optional stuff.

notes

If using leftover sushi rice just add a tiny bit of shoyu and MSG -- the mirin and pinch of salt would make it too sweet and salty. If going for something really decadent, you can add a second egg yolk once mixed. Could probably use a semi-brown haiga rice for this dish as well.
